">

This plot is used to show the differences in temperature based upon relative location to the equator. As expected, the temperature is warmer the closer to the equator with a slight decrease as you move away from the equator. The temperature does seem to be warmer in the southern hemisphere, this is probably due to the tilt in the earth's axis.
